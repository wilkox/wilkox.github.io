Hi! I'm David Wilkins, a microbial ecologist and medical student in Sydney, Australia.

My main research interest is the microbial biogeography of natural and artificial environments.

I write open source software, mostly R packages for statistical graphics. My most popular projects are the ggplot2 extensions treemapify, ggfittext, and gggenes.

I teach the courses Introduction to R Programming and Intermediate R Programming at The University of Sydney's Centre for Continuing Education.
